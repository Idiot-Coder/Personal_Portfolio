Project Overview

A Simple friendly Quiz app where users get multiple-choice questions from different topics and after selecting an option they got points and proceed further once they have selected all the answers they get a final score based on it, If they like to save their score they can save it by giving their name.

It's a fun yet knowledgeable game to enjoy with friends/family.
